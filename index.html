About Us

Look, there are a ton of incredibly intelligent or motivated or charismatic individuals out there. I am somewhere in between all of those. I am a connector, I am a stone helping you across the creek. I give you my word that I’ll give you my best effort in what I do, and that is backed by:

  • My love for my daughter. The world needs people to share their gifts and their skills. Her well-being matters, so I will lean into the cutting edge of mission-driven efficiency to improve the world for her.
  • My love for the natural world. No matter where I’ve gone building a connection with and being a protector for our planet has been at the forefront of my effort.
  • My love for my community. Whether it is in individuals or organizations I keep an eye out for who is doing good work so we can catalyze bigger impacts for those that need it. We all need those that look out for us.

I care a lot, but why should you work with me?

  • A decade of education across the lifespan, most of which is through a sustainability lens.
  • Acknowledged with a national award, “ACYCP Dr. Carol Kelly’s Newcomer Award” for the impact I have had locally.
  • Project Management Professional (PMP) certified to show that I execute with excellence in a timely and efficient manner. From small grassroots programs to large statewide projects I can serve and lead a team towards their intended goal.
  • Cross-functional experience in education, public health, and economic/workforce development
  • Self-trained and professionally acknowledged, entrepreneurial data consultant.
  • A range of experience and training in greenhouse gas accounting, environmental management, permaculture, and naturalist studies.
